Bringing Awareness through Systems for Humans Lab

About

We are interested in understanding and enhancing the intelligence, usability and processing capabilities of tiny computing devices to realize their full potential in our daily lives. Our research focuses on machine learning for Internet- of-Things, mobile and pervasive computing, cyber-physical systems (CPS), and mobile health.

Mission

Our aim is to introduce a new set of sustainable edge computers that are time-aware, inference capable, life-long learning, programmable, personalized, and user-friendly. Our works will provide sustainable and scalable sensing solutions in various application domains ranging from health care to smart agriculture.

Approach

We leverage the data processing and control layer of mobile systems and Internet of Things (IoT) by developing frameworks that (1) integrate different domains of computer science and engineering including networking, energy harvesting, and real-time systems (2) rethink machine learning and computer vision algorithms, and (3) utilize contextual and symantic diversity of data.
